Textile Care Instructions

Proper care extends the life of your textiles. Here are some tips for maintaining our eco-friendly fabrics:

  • Wash Cold: Use cold water to save energy and protect delicate fabrics.
  • Air Dry: Avoid using dryers; instead, air dry to reduce energy consumption.
  • Avoid Bleach: Use mild detergents and avoid bleach to prevent fabric damage and water contamination.
  • Iron Low: Iron on a low setting to prevent overheating and damage to fibers.
